      • Bonus Action: negate falling damage, tripple jumping distance, ignore AoO, advantage auf acrobatic checks für 1 Stunde

      • Blade dance: 2 blade flourishes pro use of blade flourish

    • Wizard:
      • Expansive Arcana: double range and radius of spells for 1 min
      • Vulnerability Induction: 1 target für 1 min lang vulnerable gegen damage type; immune -> resist; resist -> normal; normal -> vuln
